part memoir, part biography, hanif weaves in his love for one of the pillars in hip-hop into a beautifully written book that i think non-tribe fans would equally enjoy. i loved how hanif compared q-tip and phife's relationship to that of ewing and starks, and teared up a bit when he wished phife was able to see the knicks succeed before his death.
