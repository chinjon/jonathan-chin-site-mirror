>"_he speaks in your voice, american, and there's a shine in his eye that's halfway hopeful._"

i intend to read delillo's _underworld_ anyway, this novella being it's prologue, but given the recent knicks' title run and the fourth, thought it would be a timely read. 

delillo weaves through themes crowd, class and race via a singular sporting event, building up tension, hopes, anxieties and the eventual conclusion, showing how much sports (or a singular event) can affect so many lives.
