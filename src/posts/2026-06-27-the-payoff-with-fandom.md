---
title: 'the payoff with fandom'
date: '2026-06-27'
summary: 'musings of a fan, after their favorite team finally reaches the top again'
tags: ['the knicks']
---

i still don't know what to do with myself, two weeks after the fact. the knicks did it. the knicks were the last one standing. the knicks did the thing that many of us feared would never happen in our lifetime. they won. watching game five felt like a blur. 95% of the time i thought they were going back to the garden, although based on the past 2 months, i really shouldn't have. after the final 3 point hail mary from wemby and the rebound/victory hurl from og, i screamed.

the day after, i was at a gas station filling up my car, and the thought hit me, what about the next one. can they repeat? seasons after this one won't feel as anguished, no more "well maybe next year" or "lets see what they do in the offseason". it was almost a habit, or a fact of life, the knicks falling short, not a matter of if but by how much.
