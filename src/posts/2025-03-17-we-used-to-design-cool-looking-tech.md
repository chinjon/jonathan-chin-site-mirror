in my research, it occurred to me that there used to be so many creative looking phones that were released regularly.

***

now, we're just expecting the same bland slab, no physical buttons or limiting in ports, and nothing to reflect our personalities. its no surprise, even on the mainstream web, we had "spaces" that allowed us to reflect and showcase who we were, like myspace. not to say that those spaces don't exist anymore, like tumblr and personal blogs, but its not something everyone expects or are looking for.

we used to be a society that designed cool as hell technology, and with society's gradual embrace of ai, our design and identities are going the way of the smart phone slab, cookie cutter design.
